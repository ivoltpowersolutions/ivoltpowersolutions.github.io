What is a Landed Price?

The landed price is the full cost of an imported item once it arrives at your door in Nigeria. It includes: CIF value (cost + international freight + insurance) + Import Duty (varies by HS code, typically 5–20%) + VAT (7.5%) + Port handling & clearance + Local logistics (Lagos to your state) + FX risk buffer (protects against Naira movement between order and delivery). Our bot calculates all of this live. The price you see is the price you pay — no surprises.

How Export Pricing Works

Export prices are calculated live against international commodity benchmarks. Scrap copper is priced against COMEX copper futures ($/tonne). Aluminium is priced against LME aluminium. Used equipment is priced at a discount to CIF replacement cost, adjusted for condition and age. All prices are shown in both USD and NGN at the live exchange rate. Our bot command /trade export copper from=Nigeria to=UK qty=1000 gives you a live quote including freight and documentation.
